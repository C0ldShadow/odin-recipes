Beef Steak

Beef steak is a flat cut of beef, being a common serving in some restaurants, being prepared in varying degrees depending on the cut. It's also a very popular option in homes due to its high nutritional value and easy preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb soft meat cut into pieces
  • 500g red tomato
  • 500 grams of white big onion
  • 1 stick of celery
  • Half a red pepper without seeds
  • 4 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cloves of garlic peeled and crushed

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan.
  2. Add the meat and seal it on both sides.
  3. Chop the onion into slices and add it to the meat.
  4. Peel and chop the tomatoes into slices. Add to the pan with the meat.
  5. Add the spices and the soy sauce.
  6. Stir and leave the meat on the mixture.
  7. Cover and leave over medium-low heat for 15 minutes.
  8. Turn off the heat and stir the preparation.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
